# 🏏 Cricket Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview

This dashboard analyzes cricket match data to understand team performance, match trends, pitch conditions, and weather impact.

---

## 📊 Features

* Matches won by teams
* Monthly match trends
* Pitch statistics
* Weather analysis
* Team comparison
* Batting & bowling averages

## 💡 Insights

* India and England dominate in match wins
* Balanced pitches are most common
* Weather affects match frequency
* Team performance varies across conditions
